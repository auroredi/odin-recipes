Migas

Description

Migas is a quick and easy Tex-Mex egg dish, perfect for lazy Saturday morning breakfasts.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 10-12 Halved Grape Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 2 Corn Tortillas
  • 1/2 Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/4 Avocado
  • 1/4 Cup Fresh Salsa or 2 Tbsp Salsa in Jar
  • Chopped Cilantro for Garnish

    • Steps

    1. Tear corn tortillas into bite-sized pieces.
    2. Beat eggs in a small bowl until well-mixed.
    3. Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    4. Add tomatoes to oil and cook until they begin to release their juices.
    5. Add tortillas pieces to the skillet and cook until soft.
    6. Add eggs to tortillas pieces and cook, stirring occassionally.
    7. When eggs are firm, turn off heat and add cheese.
    8. Remove migas from the skillet when the cheese is melted and put on a plate to serve.
    9. Garnish with avocado slices, salsa, and cilantro.
