High Protein Overnight Oats

Description

This version of Overnight Oats is intended to suit vegans or vegetarians who are following a high-protein/high fiber diet such as The Total Body Reset.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Regular Oats
  • 1 Tbsp Chia Seeds
  • 1 Scoop Vegan Protein Powder
  • 1 Cup Soy Milk, Plain or Vanilla
  • 1/2 Cup Sliced Strawberries

Recipe Steps

  1. Place all ingredients except strawberries in a Mason jar.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Cover jar and let sit in refridgerator over night.
  4. Transfer to a bowl in the morning and top with strawberries.
  5. Enjoy!
