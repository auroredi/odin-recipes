Vegetarian Breakfast Burritos

Description

This breakfast burrito contains 3 sources of vegetarian protein: eggs, tofu, and cheese. It omits the potatoes frequently found in other breakfast burrito recipes and calls for Carb Chopper tortillas in order to reduce carbohydrates while giving it a high fiber profile.

Ingredients

  • 1 Santa Fe Tortilla Company Whole Wheat Carb Chopper Tortilla, Large
  • 1 Large Egg, Beaten
  • 4 Ounces Firm Tofu
  • 1/2 Tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Hatch Green Chile, Chopped

Recipe Steps

  1. Place all ingredients except strawberries in a Mason jar.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Cover jar and let sit in refridgerator over night.
  4. Transfer to a bowl in the morning and top with strawberries.
  5. Enjoy!
