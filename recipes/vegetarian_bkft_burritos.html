Vegetarian Breakfast Burritos

Description

This breakfast burrito contains 3 sources of vegetarian protein: eggs, tofu, and cheese. It omits the potatoes frequently found in other breakfast burrito recipes and calls for Carb Chopper tortillas in order to reduce carbohydrates while giving it a higher fiber profile.

Ingredients

  • 1 Santa Fe Tortilla Company Whole Wheat Carb Chopper Tortilla, Large
  • 1 Large Egg, Beaten
  • 4 Ounces Firm Tofu
  • 1/2 Tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Hatch Green Chile, Chopped

Recipe Steps

  1. Slice tofu as thinly as possible.
  2. Add oil and tofu to non-stick skillet and cook over medium heat.
  3. When tofu is lightly browned, add beaten egg and green chile.
  4. Continue cooking over medium heat while stirring frequently.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  6. After egg is firm, turn off heat and sprinkle cheese over egg mixture.
  7. Fold into tortilla and enjoy!
